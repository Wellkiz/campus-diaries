Campus Hustle: Balancing Studies and Work

Posted on February 28, 2025

Juggling classes, assignments, and side hustles can be tough, but with the right strategies, it's possible to excel in both!

Here are some key strategies:

  • Use a planner to manage time effectively.
  • Prioritize tasks based on deadlines and importance.
  • Take breaks to avoid burnout.
  • Communicate with employers about your schedule.

By implementing these strategies, you can successfully manage both academics and work without sacrificing one for the other.
