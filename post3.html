Surviving on a Student Budget

Posted on February 25, 2025

Managing money as a student can be challenging. Here are some practical tips:

  • Track your expenses and create a budget.
  • Cook your own meals instead of eating out.
  • Take advantage of student discounts.
  • Avoid impulse purchases.

With smart financial decisions, you can make your money last longer!
